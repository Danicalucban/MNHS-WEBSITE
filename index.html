Welcome to Muntinlupa National High School

We are thrilled to have you join our vibrant community of learners. At MNHS, we believe that education is a journey, and we are committed to providing you with the support and resources you need to achieve your goals. Whether you're interested in science, technology, engineering, the arts, or any other field, we have a wide range of academic programs and extracurricular activities to help you explore your interests and build your skills. Our dedicated faculty and staff are passionate about teaching and mentoring students, and we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where every student can thrive. We encourage you to get involved in our school community, make new friends, and take advantage of the many opportunities available to you. Thank you for choosing Muntinlupa National High School, and we look forward to working with you on your educational journey.
